Hello! My name is Jenya. My journey into the world of fashion began with modeling experience, which helped me gain a deeper understanding of the industry from the inside and develop a unique sense of style. Today, I am a passionate and creative stylist with over two years of experience in fashion design.

I know how to turn ideas into collections, combining inspiration with practical skills in clothing design and project management. My expertise covers fashion illustration, pattern making, and trend analysis. I enjoy creating not just clothes, but stories that people can wear.
